Life Tracker (Habitualize)

This project is undergoing a major rework.

An ongoing project of mine, the Life Tracker (I'm currently thinking of better names), is a elementary form of habit tracking.

Utilizing a Google Form and Google Sheets, users are given a set of major lifestyle habits to track such as hours slept or step count. Users may then choose to add any of their own “for fun” supplemental life statistics. Each night, users will fill out the Google Form which updates the spreadsheet in real time. At the end of every quarter, users will be able to check a summary of their major lifestyle statistics, as well as any supplemental statistics that they are willing to take the time to implement a formula for. Think Spotify Wrapped, but for practical aspects of your life!

If you'd like access to start your own tracking today, contact me through socials or email!

What's the Point?

Why do we even care about this data? What's the project even meant to accomplish?

2024 was not a very fun year for Matthew Han. So in 2025, he built the Life Tracker as a way to improve lifestyle through consistent check-ins and insight. The data is meant to be learned from and analyzed so that you can improve in what areas you lack progress while maintaining progress in areas you excel in.

Also, it's just fun! Imagine that life is like a videogame where after you pass away, you get to see a summary of all your life statistics (like this reddit post!) Learning more about yourself, discovering your identity, is one of life's greatest joys!
